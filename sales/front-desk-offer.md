# The Aevon Front Desk (flagship offer, decided 2026-07-05)

One product, one price, one week. This supersedes the "custom software, quote after
discovery" framing as the DEFAULT offer. Custom builds still exist as the upsell.

## The one-liner
An AI agent that runs your inbound intake end to end: it answers and qualifies every
inquiry, books the appointment, files every lead into a simple pipeline, and follows
up with the ones that go quiet. Nothing sends without your approval.

## Terms
- $1,500 flat setup. Live inside a week.
- $150/month: hosting, monitoring, AI costs, and tuning as your business changes.
- You own the software. Cancel the monthly any time and self-host if you want.
- Founding-client rate (first 2 clients): $900 setup, monthly locked at $100 for year one,
  in exchange for a testimonial, a case study, and 1-2 introductions. Deposit still required.

## Why this against Gemini/Copilot (the objection to expect)
Gmail and Outlook AI help the OWNER write a reply when they open an email. The Front Desk
is a worker, not a writing aid:
- qualifies each inquiry against THEIR criteria (budget, timeline, service area, fit)
- books against THEIR calendar and rules
- sends THEIR documents (packages, NDAs, intake forms) at the right step
- files everything into a pipeline board and chases no-replies
- weekly digest of what came in, what booked, what stalled
Platform AI stops at the inbox wall. The moat is workflow integration plus a local human
who sets it up and tunes it. Never pitch it as "AI that reads your email."

## Delivery reality (why $0 marginal cost)
It is the stack Aevon already runs on its own inbox: reply-drafting agent, qualification,
CRM board, tracking, booking links, follow-up chasing. Per client it is configuration
(their services, voice, calendar, documents, pipeline stages) plus 2 weeks of tuning.
The $150/mo covers API usage with margin.

## Sales motion
1. Demo first, everywhere: the 90-second reel in person, on LinkedIn, in partner pitches.
2. Cold email (autopilot only): offers the demo, names the terms in follow-up 2.
3. Warm channels are the real engine: referral partners (bookkeepers, accountants, MSPs),
   personal network, local groups. 10 conversations/week target.
4. Proof: dogfood case study from Aevon's/Tech Neighbour's own inboxes with real numbers.

## Scope guardrails (protect the week and the price)
Included: one inbox, one calendar, one pipeline board, up to 3 document templates,
one brand voice. More brands/inboxes/integrations = custom quote (see quote-template.md).
