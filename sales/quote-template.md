# Quote Template (with recurring revenue)

The structure that keeps the "you own it" promise AND builds monthly recurring revenue.
Pricing stays variable and value-based, quoted per project after a discovery call.
All figures CAD. These are floors and ranges, not a published price list.

## The key idea
The client OWNS the software. The monthly fee is for OPERATING the agent, which is real,
ongoing work: the AI model usage (a genuine per-use cost), monitoring so it never silently
breaks and drops leads, tuning as their business changes, and support. This is honest and
distinct from a SaaS subscription, because they could self-host if they wanted. Most will not
want to, which is where the recurring revenue comes from.

## Two ways to buy (offer both, expect most to pick Managed)

### Option A — Self-hosted (the purist "you own it")
- One-time build only. They host it, they run it, it is fully theirs.
- No monthly fee.
- Reality: most SMBs do not want to manage an AI agent. Offering this makes the "you own it"
  promise true and makes Option B feel like a choice, not a trap.

### Option B — Managed (recommended, this is the MRR)
- One-time build, discounted slightly versus self-hosted, PLUS monthly agent operations.
- You host, monitor, tune, and support it. They just use it.
- This is what creates a real business instead of a one-time-build treadmill.

## One-time build (value-priced by scope)
Quote after discovery. Raise the floor: never quote serious custom work at the old $1,500.

- Starter agent (one workflow, e.g. inbound inquiry handling): from $2,500 to $4,500
- Core system (a few workflows + a simple desk/CRM view): $5,000 to $9,000
- Full system (multi-brand, integrations, multiple agents): $9,000 to $18,000+

Anchor on the value: hours saved per week, leads that stop slipping, deals not missed.
Tie the number to their numbers, not to a cost-plus estimate.

## Monthly agent operations (the recurring tier)
Covers model/API usage, monitoring and uptime, tuning as their workflow changes, and support.

- Lite (low volume, one agent): $150 to $250 / mo
- Standard (steady volume, monitoring + light tuning): $250 to $450 / mo
- Plus (high volume or multiple agents, priority support): $450 to $900 / mo

Position it plainly: "The software is yours. This keeps the agent running, watched, and
improving. You can also take it in-house any time." That honesty is the differentiator.

## Founding client pricing (see founding-client-offer.md)
- One-time build: 40 to 50 percent off
- Monthly: locked founding rate, e.g. one tier down, for the first year

## Always
- Deposit before build (50 percent is normal for custom work). Never pay-after-satisfaction.
- Free discovery call to scope. The quote comes after, not before.
- Put it in a simple written proposal: scope, price, monthly, timeline, deposit. One page.
